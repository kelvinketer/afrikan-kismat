Who We Are

More Than Just
A Safari Company

We are a collective of local guides, conservationists, and tech innovators dedicated to showing you the real Africa.

We Don't Just Observe Nature,
We Protect It.

Afrikan Kismat Expeditions was born from a simple idea: that tourism should benefit the people and wildlife of East Africa, not just exploit them.

Unlike mass-market tour operators, we are 100% locally owned and operated. Every trip you take with us directly supports local community projects, from water conservation in the Mara to education initiatives in Nairobi.

Our Core Values

Community First

We believe safari tourism must empower locals. We hire exclusively from local communities and ensure fair wages for all staff.

Sustainability

Leave no trace. We partner with eco-friendly lodges and practice strict waste management to keep our parks pristine for generations.

Authenticity

No "tourist traps." We take you off the beaten path to experience the raw, unfiltered beauty of the African bush.
