Who We Are

African Kismat Expeditions is a professionally managed safari and travel company based in Nairobi, Kenya, specializing in tailor-made and scheduled safari experiences across Kenya and East Africa. We design intelligent, value-driven journeys for travelers who seek authenticity, comfort, and meaningful engagement with Africa’s landscapes, wildlife, and cultures.

Our name Kismat—meaning destiny—reflects our belief that every journey is more than travel; it is a purposeful encounter. We serve leisure travelers, small groups, families, and corporate clients with a strong focus on ethical tourism, transparent pricing, and reliable operations.
